old man of the woods

solo project of richmond-based songwriter & producer miranda elliott.

wrapped in a warm fog, yearning vocals float through waves of synth and heartbeats of bass, forming a world equally intimate and organic.
when miranda grew up, she wanted to be a tree. the only child of a landscape painter, the forest felt like kin. one steamy summer afternoon at her dad’s blue ridge cabin, she met the old man of the woods.
for half a decade, miranda had been hoarding dark, introspective songs. that synthesis was mirrored in the mushroom before her - that alchemy of shit into sustenance. under the old man’s guise, she was free to unfurl.
dissolve is old man of the woods’ debut ep, made during a month of rain and bad bout of bronchitis. everything clouded and waterlogged, the boundary between environment and self began to fade. miranda found comfort in letting herself dissolve, and hopes you will too.
